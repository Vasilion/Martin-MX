Equipment Requirements

The following items are required in order to ride at Martin MX Park. If you enter the track without these items, you will be asked to exit the track until you can meet the requirements. It is the riders responsibility to ensure these requirements are met prior to entering Martin MX Park.

  • DOT Approved Helmet
  • Goggles
  • Long Pants
  • Long Sleeve Shirt
  • Boots Over Ankles
  • No Kickstands
  • No Training Wheels

Track Rules And Policies

Sign in is required prior to parking

Prior to entering the facility everyone in your vehicle is required to check in and sign the proper liability waiver forms. This includes spectators.

No Drugs Or Alcohol

Alcohol is not permitted on the track premises. Additionally, riding while under the influence of any drugs or alcohol is strictly prohibited. If Martin MX Park staff suspect that any riders are under the influence of drugs or alcohol, they will be asked to leave without refund. In some circumstances, we will contact the proper authorities if necessary. Martin MX Park takes this policy very seriously and will be enforced. The safety of our riders is paramount.

Obnoxious/Aggressive/Harassing Behavior Is Not Tolerated

Martin MX Park values the enjoyment of all our riders & spectators. While we all like to let loose and have fun, loud and obnoxious behavior that disrupts the enjoyment of fellow riders & spectators will not be tolerated. Additionally, any rider(s) or spectators that display any aggressive actions towards fellow riders or spectators will be asked to leave. This also includes any harassing behavior towards any of the Martin MX Park staff.

Spectators

Spectators are welcomed, and encouraged to come watch. All non-riding spectators ages 5+ are subject to a $5 entry fee and required to sign a waiver prior to entering the facility.

Early Entry Welcomed

We welcome all riders that show up early. Showing up early will allow you to beat the opening traffic, and time to set up in your favorite spot. However, showing up early does not mean the track is open early.

Tents/Canopies Allowed

We encourage the use of canopies and tents while riding at Martin MX Park. We just ask that you be courteous of other visitors when you are choosing your set up location. Use only the space you actually need.

No Riding Prior To Opening

Riding the track prior to opening hours is strictly prohibited and will get you a quick escort off the grounds. Our staff operates equipment on the track right up until our designated opening hour. This policy is in place to ensure YOUR safety as well as our staff's safety.

Pets Are Allowed

We allow riders & spectators to bring their pets. However they MUST be on a leash at ALL TIMES. Martin MX staff reserves the right to disallow any loud or aggressive pets. If you feel that your pet might be this way, we do have remote parking areas to accommodate any potential issues. Additionally, please clean up after your pet.

Cash Payments Only

Currently, we are only equipped to handle cash at our practice facility. However, future items such as Martin MX Park apparel and accessories will be available for online purchase via credit card. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

Concessions

Currently, we offer water ($1) and monster energy drinks ($2) which are available in the check-in building. We encourage you to bring your own food & water. However there is a gas station and Subway located 1/2 mile from our track.

Re-Entry

If you need to temporarily leave the track for any reason, upon re-entry you must present your wristband to the check-in attendant.

No UAVs/Drones

While we are huge fans of the footage drones can capture, their use during open practices is strictly prohibited unless approved by Martin MX Park.

Camping is not allowed

At this time we do not allow any camping.

No fires allowed
No Pit Bikes

Special Event Policies

Limited Track Access

During special events, access to the inside areas of the track will be limited to approved individuals only. Parents of riders (no guardians) in the small bike classes are the only exception to this rule.

Spectator Fees

There is a $5 dollar spectator fee in place for special events.

Parking

Parking remains free during special events.
