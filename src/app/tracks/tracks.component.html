Our Tracks

The Main Motocross Track

Our main practice track is configured for riders of all skill levels. It is a very rider friendly course, where the average rider feels very comfortable and the faster riders are still challenged.

The main track features roughly 22 turns and features a stadium triple, multiple tabletops and tied together with rollers and a nice rhythm section. Riders will the track offers a nice balance of features. The main track is also undergoing constant changes such as new jumps, turns and more.

In addition to regular tweaks to the track layout, most of our upgrades involve giving you the best possible ride experience, such as bringing in new dirt, installing and upgrading watering capabilities, and much, much more.

The Junior Track

Our Jr. track is perfect for the inexperienced rider. The track is made from a hard pack surface that is great for the inexperienced rider. The Jr. track features many right, left and off camber turns that help build your young motocross riders skill level. The track also features all tabletops for the jumps, no doubles or triples!

At Martin MX Park, we believe in youngsters riding at an early age. These young riders are the future of the motocross industry. With that said, their safety is our top priority while learning their skills on one of our practice tracks.
